SUBWAY DESKTOP HELP
===================

HOW TO SEARCH
-------------
1. Click the Subway icon in your menu bar.
2. Type a station name or ID in the search box.
   - Example: "14th", "Union", "Canal"
   - Searching "14th" will find "14 St", "14 St-Union Sq", etc.
3. Click on the station in the list to update the desktop widget.

HOW TO QUIT
-----------
1. Click the Subway icon in your menu bar.
2. Click the "Quit" button at the bottom of the menu.

ABOUT
-----
This app displays the next 2 trains for your selected station, sorted by shortest arrival time.
The window sits on your desktop background behind all other windows.
Data provided by: wheresthefuckingtrain.com
